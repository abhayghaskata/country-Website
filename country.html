Why USA?

  1. This country comprises of world class universities offering good quality education in niche subjects.
  2. Excellent for research and technological development.
  3. USA has many options for students with average profile.
  4. The Strongest economy. More than 1/5th of global fortune 500 companies originate from USA.
  5. Scholarship opportunities in 1 of the costliest areas to study.
  6. Work along with study option available so does not need to depend on family or loan for at least living expenses.
  7. Research opportunities available related to niche segments of various subjects.
  8. Academic flexibility: students are given opportunity to explore through wide spectrum of subjects during their course of study.
  9. System is not rigid about majors and minors.
  10. Good and multilingual campus life.
  11. High standard of living.
  12. Globally recognized degree.
  13. Higher package when you want to pursue your career in your home country as compared to your peers.
  14. Also possible to go without IELTS.
